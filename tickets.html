For Companies!

New Opportunity

Give your colleagues a big laugh on your conference, seminar or party! Everything is improvised, so it will be a completely unique show just for you!

Contact Us

Are you a company and wants to give your colleagues more joy in life? Then hire us. We improvise based on your suggestions and your reality at your workspace. Maybe you are wondering what your workplace would look like if it were a musical? Or what do your management meetings really look like with a twist of comedy in it? With warmth and humour, we describe your work and the topics that are relevant for you!
